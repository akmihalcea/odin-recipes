떡볶이 (Tteokbokki)

Description

떡볶이 (Tteokbokki) is rice cakes traditionally seasoned with spicy chili paste called 고추장 (Gochujang). It is a versatile dish by adding different toppings, types of rice cakes, or changing the seasoning. Many street vendors sell 떡볶이 (Tteokbokki) which is a crowd favorite especially amongst students.

The following is a simple bare-bones version which can be modified to suit taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup or 5-6 oz. 떡 (Rice Cakes)
  • 3/4 cup Water
  • 2 tsp. 고추장 (Gochujang)
  • 1 tsp. Soy Sauce
  • 1 Boiled Egg (optional)
  • 1 tsp. Sesame Seeds (optional)
  • 1 Chopped Scallion (optional)

Steps

  1. Defrost 떡 (Rice Cakes) if frozen.
  2. Add 3/4 cup of Water, 2 tsp 고추장 (Gochujang), and 1 tsp Soy Sauce to a pot or pan and bring to a boil on medium high heat.
  3. Add 떡 (Rice Cakes) and lower heat. Simmer for 8-10 minutes or until sauce thickens. Stir occasionally to avoid 떡 (Rice Cakes) sticking to bottom.
  4. Serve with Sesame Seeds (optional) or Chopped Scallion (optional).
